Who We Are

MetalVyapar is India's first B2B e-commerce platform for metal scrap. We offer a one-stop solution for buyers and sellers to trade in variety of ferrous scrap.

While the industry has grown rapidly over the past decade, there has been limited digitalization. The high volatility in prices and ever-changing demand/supply dynamics have exerted tremendous pressure on all players.

As a seller

Register with us and skip trade intermediaries. We connect you directly to recyclers and smelters pan-India and offer you:
a. Best rate in real-time
b. Hassle-free online trade
c. Access to larger buyer audience

As a buyer

You can procure material directly from MSMEs without worrying about quality. Our registered sellers offer various varieties of MS scrap such as HR-CR (Low Carbon), HR-CR (High Carbon), Melting, Piece to Piece, Turning & Turning Bundle.
a. Procure larger quantities
b. Standardized product quality
